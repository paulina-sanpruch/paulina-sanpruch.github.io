Hi, I'm Paulina. I live in Poland, Cracow. I've been drawing since childhood. Over time, I was discovering different techniques, from pencil sketching to computer graphics.

Education

I have finished a high school of fine arts in Kielce. My paintings were presented on a few exhibitions, including my own. I studied architecture and urban planning at the Cracow University of Technology and have a MSc degree in architectural engineering. I learned there principles of design and construction, as well as application of 3D computer graphics in visualisations.

Experience

After graduation I decided to develop myself in the direction of computer graphics. I worked in a company producing mobile applications for children on tablets and smartphones. My responsibilities included 2D animations, drawing illustrations and designing characters to be used in educational fables for kids.

Parallel to the full-time job, I have been doing freelance work for marketing agencies and engineering firms: illustrations, logo design and technical drawings.

Technical skills

I am well versed in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, AutoCAD, 3D Studio Max.

Hobbies

My hobbies include latin dance and poetry.
